CodeCoach

CodeCoach is a peer to peer coaching program for professional developers.

Junior developers who struggle with a certain technique or technology can grow with the help of like minded people.

With CodeCoach everything is quick and easy: just register as a Coach or request a coaching session on this website, and it will organize a private coaching session remote or f2f.

It is action that creates motivationformat_quote
Steve Backley

Become a Coach

To help others and increase your potential

If you feel strong in a technology and want to help others, sign up as a coach. You will strengthen your knowledge in this subject, you will develop new skills and above all you will feel useful. Your commitment will be recognized and rewarded.

Benefit from coaching

To improve your skills, and keep growing

If you have difficulties with a technology and you want to progress, make an appointment for peer 2 peer coaching, and benefit from the experience of another software developer, who understands your concrete needs. Your willingness to progress will be recognized.
